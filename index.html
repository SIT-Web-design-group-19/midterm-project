Our Japanese Journey

Dennis

Dennis, a 23-year-old with a background in system operations and application development, took a six-month adventure to Japan to study software engineering. As an anime fan, he was excited to explore the country, make new friends, and improve his communication skills. Once in Japan, Dennis joined the Shibaura Institute of Technology, where he immersed himself in Japanese culture. He visited historical sites, temples, and museums, and experienced Akihabara, Tokyo's anime district. He also enjoyed tasting various Japanese dishes throughout his stay. Throughout his six months, Dennis improved his communication skills, made lifelong friends, and gained a deeper appreciation for Japanese culture. By the end of his time in Japan, Dennis had experienced personal growth and cherished the unforgettable memories, knowing he'd want to return to Japan in the future.
